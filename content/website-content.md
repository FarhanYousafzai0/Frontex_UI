## SECTION 6: What I Won't Do (`id="values"`)

**Section heading:**
What I won't do.

**I won't disappear on you.**
You'll know what I'm working on, what's done, and what's next. If something is blocked, you'll hear about it immediately — not two weeks later. Ghosting is the #1 complaint about freelancers. I don't do that.

**I won't build something fragile.**
Speed matters, but not at the cost of quality. I ship fast because I plan well and use the right tools — not because I skip testing, ignore edge cases, or write spaghetti code. What I build should still work 12 months from now.

**I won't waste your money.**
Every hour I spend should be traceable to a result. I scope tightly, prioritize ruthlessly, and tell you when something isn't worth building. If a feature doesn't serve your users or your business, I'll push back — respectfully, but clearly.

**I won't overstay.**
If the project is done and you don't need me anymore, I'll say so. If I'm not the right fit for what you need, I'll tell you that in the first conversation — not after you've already paid.
